# SharX — Supplement, Herb & Rx (drug) interaXtion Checker

> **Live app:** [https://sharx-app.github.io/SharX/](https://sharx-app.github.io/SharX/)

SharX is an AI-powered Progressive Web App (PWA) that checks interactions between drugs, supplements, and herbs. Built by a Master of Pharmacy, it combines clinical knowledge with modern AI to provide fast, accurate, and understandable interaction reports.

---

## What it does

Enter up to three substances (prescription drugs, OTC medications, supplements, or herbs) and SharX returns:

- **Severity rating** — MAJOR / MODERATE / MINOR / NONE
- **Mechanism of interaction** — what happens at the pharmacological level
- **Clinical relevance** — what it means for the patient
- **Recommendation** — what to do about it

---

## Key features

- **AI-powered analysis** — uses Claude AI (Anthropic) for clinical-grade interaction checking
- **Multilingual results** — English, Serbian, German, Spanish, French, Italian
- **PDF report download** — one click export for patients or healthcare providers
- **Search history** — previous checks saved locally
- **PWA / installable** — works on desktop and mobile, can be installed as an app
- **No login required** — privacy-first, nothing stored on servers

---

## Why SharX?

Most interaction checkers are either too technical for patients or too simplified for clinicians. SharX bridges that gap — it gives clinically meaningful information in plain language, fast.
